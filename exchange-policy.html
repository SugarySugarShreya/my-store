EXCHANGE POLICY

Exchange Policy

At Soft Launch, many of our products are custom-made and personalized specifically for each customer. Because of this, we do not offer refunds.

However, we are happy to provide an exchange if:

  • Your product arrives damaged during transit.
  • You receive the wrong product.
  • You receive an item with a manufacturing defect.

To request an exchange

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Provide your order number.
  • Share clear photographs of the product and packaging.

Once approved, we'll arrange a replacement or exchange.

Exchanges are not available for

  • Minor colour variations due to screen settings.
  • Incorrect information submitted by the customer (such as spelling, names, addresses, or uploaded photos).
  • Normal wear and tear after delivery.
  • Change of mind after purchase.
